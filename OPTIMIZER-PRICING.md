# Optimizer Pricing Model

**Effective Date:** 2026-04-16  
**Status:** Finalized  
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-16

---

## Pricing Philosophy

Optimizer pricing is based on **client annual revenue** because smaller businesses typically require less optimization complexity than larger ones. We charge a **base retainer + performance fee** to align incentives: you win when clients win.

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Tier 1: Under $1M Annual Revenue
- **Base Retainer:** $600/mo
- **Performance Fee:** 10% of new client revenue
- **Flat Fee Alternative:** $800-1,000/mo (12+ month commitment only)

**When to use:** Local service businesses (plumbers, HVAC, electricians, small retail)

### Tier 2: $1M-$3M Annual Revenue
- **Base Retainer:** $850/mo
- **Performance Fee:** 12% of new client revenue
- **Flat Fee Alternative:** $1,500-1,800/mo (12+ month commitment only)

**When to use:** Mid-size service businesses, regional companies

### Tier 3: $3M+ Annual Revenue
- **Base Retainer:** $1,200/mo
- **Performance Fee:** 15% of new client revenue
- **Flat Fee Alternative:** $2,000-2,400/mo (12+ month commitment only)

**When to use:** Large agencies, professional services (law firms, accounting), national brands

---

## What's Included in Every Tier

1. **Directory Optimization** — 30 high-authority, industry-specific directories
2. **Content Optimization** — Answer Capsules + website copy (via N8N + manual edits)
3. **Monthly AI Scans** — Tracker visibility monitoring (premium included)
4. **Ongoing Updates** — Content refreshes every 3 months (keeps AI citations fresh)
5. **Hands-On Management** — Jake and Joey handle all strategy and execution

---

## How Performance Fees Work

### Definition
"New client revenue" = Revenue from clients/customers who found the business via AI search (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity).

### Measurement
1. **Client Survey/Form** — New customers asked: "How did you find us?" with option "AI search (ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini/Perplexity)"
2. **Tracker Verification** — Monthly, validate that AI visibility is actually improving in Tracker
3. **Monthly Reporting** — Client reports estimated new revenue from AI leads
4. **Invoice & Payment** — You invoice for: base retainer + performance fee

### Example (Tier 1 - Bluecollar)
```
Client gains 8 new customers from AI search
Average contract value: $2,000 per customer
New revenue from AI: 8 × $2,000 = $16,000/month

Your payment:
  Base retainer: $600
  Performance: 10% × $16,000 = $1,600
  Total: $2,200/month

Client net benefit: $16,000 - $2,200 = $13,800/month profit
Client ROI: 23x return on your service
```

### Example (Tier 3 - Law Firm)
```
Client gains 3 new cases from AI search
Average case value: $50,000
New revenue from AI: 3 × $50,000 = $150,000/month

Your payment:
  Base retainer: $1,200
  Performance: 15% × $150,000 = $22,500
  Total: $23,700/month

Client net benefit: $150,000 - $23,700 = $126,300/month profit
Client ROI: 6.3x return on your service
```

## Negotiation Guidelines

### When to Offer Flat Fee
- Client prefers budget certainty (doesn't want to track/report new revenue)
- Complex tracking situation (hard for them to measure AI-sourced leads)
- High-confidence relationship (multi-year commitment likely)

### Negotiation Rules
1. **Floor:** Flat fee must equal or exceed your conservative estimate of performance upside
2. **Commitment:** Require 12+ month minimum for flat fee arrangements
3. **No more than 20% discount** from your starting ask without executive agreement (Jake or Joey approval)
4. **Document everything** in contract (which tier, which fee structure, what's included)

### Flat Fee Minimums
- **Tier 1:** $800-1,000/mo (don't go below $800)
- **Tier 2:** $1,500-1,800/mo (don't go below $1,500)
- **Tier 3:** $2,000-2,400/mo (don't go below $2,000)

---

## Service Duration & Contracts

- **Minimum contract:** 3 months (allows validation of hypothesis)
- **Recommended:** 6-12 months (time for AI re-indexing and results)
- **Month-to-month after:** Acceptable once client sees results
- **Performance fees:** Paid monthly, recurring as long as those clients stay active

---

## Key Assumptions

1. **Baseline:** Assumes 8-6 hours/month of hands-on work per client initially
2. **Efficiency:** Work hours drop as Jake/Joey get comfortable with process
3. **Directory cost:** $100/mo per client (factored into base retainer)
4. **Tracker:** Free to all clients (premium included in retainer)
5. **N8N:** ~$30/mo allocated across client base
