# Surven GEO Agency Strategy
## Building an Agency Alongside the SaaS Platform (2026-04-09)

---

## Business Model

Surven operates on **two parallel business models:**

1. **Self-Serve SaaS** — Businesses subscribe directly to use Surven (future revenue stream)
2. **GEO Agency** — You sell optimization services to businesses using Surven as your internal operating system

The agency model is the immediate revenue driver. Surven is your competitive advantage and moat.

---

## How the GEO Agency Works

### Step 1: Prospect Outreach
Reach out to local service businesses (dentists, plumbers, lawyers, marketing agencies, etc.) with a simple pitch:

**"When someone asks ChatGPT 'best plumber in Hartford CT' — does your business get mentioned? We track that and help you show up more often in AI."**

### Step 2: Initial Audit (Free)
1. Use Surven to run an AI visibility scan on their business
2. Show them where they appear (and where they don't) across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity
3. Compare them to competitors
4. Provide a simple report with findings

### Step 3: Sell the Service
Based on audit results, pitch them a GEO optimization package:

- **Starter ($500/month)** — Monthly scans, recommendations
- **Growth ($1500/month)** — Monthly scans + optimization consulting
- **Enterprise ($5000+/month)** — Custom tracking, dedicated account manager, implementation support

### Step 4: Implementation & Tracking
1. Track their AI visibility monthly via Surven
2. Provide optimization recommendations (content updates, business listing improvements, etc.)
3. Show them trending data month-over-month
4. Upsell white-label reports or custom dashboards

### Step 5: Client Lock-in
- They become dependent on your monthly Surven reports
- Surven is **proprietary** — they can't get this data elsewhere
- You build long-term relationships with recurring revenue

✅ **Scalability** — Can manage 50-100 clients with minimal overhead  
✅ **Competitive moat** — Your custom Surven platform = proprietary advantage

### For Clients

✅ **Competitive intelligence** — See where competitors show up  
✅ **Actionable data** — Clear recommendations on where to improve

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Month 1: Test & Learn
- Target 5-10 local service businesses (your city first)
- Run free audits, get feedback
- Refine pitch and service offering
- Goal: Sign 2-3 paid clients

### Month 2-3: Narrow Focus
- Double down on best-performing vertical (e.g., dentists)
- Use client results as case studies
- Target businesses with obvious gaps (not showing up in AI)
- Goal: 10-15 paying clients

### Month 4-6: Scale
- Expand to adjacent verticals (lawyers, plumbers, etc.)
- Build simple case study page / testimonials
- Partner with industry associations if possible
- Goal: 30-50 paying clients

### Month 7-12: Systematize
- Create repeatable process (audit → proposal → implementation → reporting)
- Build team (potentially hire 1 person to help with client work)
- Develop industry-specific packages
- Goal: 50-100 clients, $25k-50k/month MRR

## Operational Playbook

### Weekly Client Cadence
- **Monday:** Run automated scans for all clients (already scheduled via Surven)
- **Tuesday-Wednesday:** Analyze results, identify trends
- **Thursday-Friday:** Send monthly reports to clients, schedule 1:1 calls with key accounts

### Monthly Client Call
1. Review latest scan results (5 min)
2. Compare to last month + competitors (5 min)
3. Discuss 2-3 optimization recommendations (10 min)
4. Next steps (5 min)

**Time per call:** 25 minutes  
**With 100 clients:** ~50-60 calls/month = ~1-2 hours/week (if doing 1:1s for all)

**Optimization:** 
- Group clients by vertical for efficiency
- Create templated reports (Looker Studio dashboards)
- 1:1 calls only for top-tier clients ($2k+/month)
- Group calls or webinars for others

### Client Onboarding (1-2 hours per client)
1. Free audit scan (15 min)
2. Initial proposal meeting (30 min)
3. Setup in Surven (15 min)
4. First report delivery (10 min)

## Key Metrics to Track

### Business Health
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)** — How much you spend to get each client
- **LTV (Lifetime Value)** — How much each client is worth over time
- **Churn Rate** — % of clients leaving per month
- **MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)** — Total revenue from subscriptions
- **Gross Margin** — Revenue minus direct costs (API, tools)

### Client Success
- **AI Visibility Trend** — Are their mentions increasing?
- **Competitor Rank** — Are they beating competitors?
- **Feature Usage** — Which Surven features do they use most?
- **NPS (Net Promoter Score)** — Would they recommend you?

### Product (Surven) Metrics
- **Scan Reliability** — Do scans complete successfully?
- **API Latency** — How fast do results come back?
- **Feature Adoption** — Which Surven features do clients request?
- **Data Quality** — Are results accurate and trustworthy?

## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: "AI visibility doesn't matter to my customers"
**Mitigation:** Target verticals where it clearly does (search-driven industries)

### Risk 2: Low churn (clients don't stay long)
**Mitigation:** Build strong relationships, deliver real results, track metrics closely

### Risk 3: Competitors figure out the model
**Mitigation:** Build fast, get customers locked in, improve Surven continuously

### Risk 4: API costs spike unexpectedly
**Mitigation:** Monitor costs per client, build efficiency into scan logic, optimize prompts

### Risk 5: Clients think "I could build this myself"
**Mitigation:** Maintain competitive moat, add one-off features, own the relationship
